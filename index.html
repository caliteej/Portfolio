Hello, I am TJ Kinion...

A web consultant living in Newport Beach, CA. My love of technology began during my 4 years of work in the IT Staffing industry for Insight Global. After deciding a change was in order I traveled to Southeast Asia and upon returning pivoted to the development side by attending LearningFuze.

Technology is a passion for me; I wake up to podcasts, spend mornings reading newsletters, embrace the challenges that come with each project, love talking about it with friends, and even dream about it while sleeping.

How I can help you

Web Development

Having just completed the LearningFuze Program I can make your site pop. Whether you need something from scratch or just some adjustments, I'm here for you. And just as everyone carries a cell phone, all of my work is mobile responsive.

Design

"It's the little details that are vital" - John Wooden.

When it comes to appearance, layout, and user experience I stress over the little things so that you can reap the rewards. Some people accept something being "good enough", I will not stop until we have something that both of us agree is great.

Sales & Marketing

With 4 years of professional sales experience, a year of marketing, and an education from one of the top 3 business schools in the country my background combines to create a dangerous combination when it comes to improving your company's bottom line.

Project Management

With experience spanning the business and technical units of an organization I bring a project background that allows internal teams to do what they do best while providing the support needed to execute flawlessly. Whether it's planning the project from Day 1 or jumping into the fire at the end I know how to deliver results and more importantly manage difficult situations.
